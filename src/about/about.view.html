Campfire

About us



Campfire is a pure-text blogging tool that focuses upon content and empowers you to share your stories, emotions, and experiences using the power of language.



By signing up, you can:


  • Write a post and share you story
  • View posts by users on any topic
  • Search for posts on your favourite topics
  • Customise how the app looks to your hearts content
  • Express yourself with stories, a profile, and images

So join us and start telling your stories around the campfire, today!
